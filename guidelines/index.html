Community Guidelines

Community Guidelines for Wisers -- the rules for participating in our wealth-building community.

Last updated: 3 April 2026 · Version: 2.0 · ICO Registration: ZB973909

Summary: Wisers is a platform for wealth builders. Be respectful, share genuine value, do not spam, do not harass others, and do not post illegal content. Violations result in content removal, suspension, or permanent ban.

1. Our Mission

Wisers is a social platform built for people who are building wealth -- entrepreneurs, investors, freelancers, side-hustlers, and anyone on their financial journey. Our goal is to create a focused, respectful space where people share knowledge, ask questions, and grow together.

These guidelines apply to all content and interactions on Wisers, including posts, comments, messages, community discussions, profiles, and media uploads.

2. Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with respect, even when you disagree.
  • Engage constructively. Criticism is welcome; personal attacks are not.
  • Do not harass, bully, threaten, or intimidate other users.
  • Do not use slurs, hate speech, or language that degrades others based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or nationality.
  • Do not post content that encourages self-harm or suicide.

3. Share Genuine Value

  • Share tips, experiences, lessons learned, and resources that help others.
  • Ask genuine questions. The community is here to help.
  • Give credit where it is due. If you are sharing someone else's work or ideas, attribute them.
  • Celebrate your wins and others' wins.

4. No Spam or Self-Promotion Without Context

  • Do not post repetitive promotional content or links without adding genuine value.
  • Self-promotion is allowed when it is relevant, transparent, and accompanied by context (e.g., sharing a product you built with a genuine explanation of the journey).
  • Do not use automated tools to create accounts, post content, or follow/unfollow users.
  • Do not post affiliate links disguised as organic recommendations.

5. No Financial Fraud or Misleading Claims

  • Do not promote get-rich-quick schemes, pyramid schemes, or Ponzi schemes.
  • Do not present opinions as professional financial advice. Always make clear when you are sharing personal experience versus qualified advice.
  • Do not post fake income screenshots, fabricated results, or misleading earnings claims.
  • Do not solicit money from other users under false pretences.

6. Illegal Content

You must not post or share:

  • Content that is illegal under the laws of England and Wales or the laws of the user's jurisdiction
  • Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activity
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) -- we report all such content to the National Crime Agency and law enforcement immediately
  • Non-consensual intimate images (including deepfakes)
  • Content that incites violence or terrorism
  • Content that facilitates the sale of illegal drugs, weapons, or stolen goods
  • Content that infringes copyright or trademark rights

7. Privacy and Personal Information

  • Do not share other people's personal information without their consent (doxxing).
  • Do not post private conversations, emails, or messages without permission.
  • Do not impersonate another person or entity.
  • Do not create fake accounts or misleading profiles.

8. Media Content

  • Do not post sexually explicit content.
  • Do not post excessively violent or graphic content.
  • Images and videos must be your own or used with permission. Respect copyright.
  • Do not upload malware, viruses, or files designed to harm other users' devices.

9. Communities

  • Stay on topic within each community. Post content relevant to the community's focus.
  • Respect community-specific rules set by community moderators.
  • Do not cross-post the same content to multiple communities simultaneously.

10. Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the Report button on any post, comment, or profile.
  • Email info@balancewises.io with details of the violation.
  • For urgent safety issues (threats, CSAM, terrorism), email security@wisrs.com.

All reports are reviewed by our team. We aim to review reports within 24 hours.

11. Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

SeverityAction
Minor (first offence)Content removed, warning issued
Repeat minor offencesTemporary suspension (7 days)
Serious violationImmediate account suspension (30 days)
Severe violation (illegal content, CSAM, threats)Permanent ban and report to law enforcement

We reserve the right to take any action we deem appropriate to protect the safety and integrity of our community. Enforcement decisions are made at our sole discretion.

12. Appeals

If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you may appeal by emailing info@balancewises.io with the subject "Moderation Appeal". Include your username and a description of why you believe the decision was incorrect. We will review appeals within 14 days.

13. Changes

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. The current version will always be available at balancewises.io/guidelines/. Significant changes will be announced on the platform.
