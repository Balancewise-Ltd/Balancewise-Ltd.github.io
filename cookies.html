Cookie Policy

What cookies we use on balancewises.io, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

Last updated: 28 February 2026  ·  Version: 1.0

In plain English: We use essential cookies to make the site work (dark mode preference), and analytics cookies to understand how people use our site. We ask for your consent before placing analytics cookies. You can change your mind at any time.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit — such as your preferences or how you use the site.

Cookies can be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (remain on your device for a set period).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration Set By
bwt-theme Essential Remembers your dark / light mode preference Persistent (localStorage) balancewises.io
_ga Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes unique users 2 years Google
_ga_* Analytics Google Analytics — maintains session state 2 years Google
bwt-consent Essential Remembers your cookie consent choice 12 months balancewises.io

3. Cookie Categories Explained

Essential   These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly. They do not collect personal information and cannot be disabled without affecting how the site works.

Analytics   These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting information anonymously. We only set these cookies with your consent. They help us improve our site.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyse website traffic and improve user experience. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at our site (search engine, direct, referral)
  • Your approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type and browser

This information is anonymised — Google Analytics does not identify you personally. We have enabled IP anonymisation on our Google Analytics setup.

Google's own privacy policy applies to data processed by Google Analytics: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to:

  • Accept all cookies — including analytics cookies
  • Accept essential only — decline analytics cookies

You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site, which will show the consent banner again.

6. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

You can also control cookies directly through your browser settings. Here are links to instructions for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Samsung Internet

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.

7. Opt Out of Google Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at balancewises.io/cookies.html.
