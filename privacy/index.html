3. What Data We Collect

3.1 Account Data (Wisers, BSCAN, Console)

6. How We Store and Protect Your Data

6.1 Infrastructure

  • Our servers are hosted on DigitalOcean (data centres in London, UK and Frankfurt, EU)
  • Media files (images, videos, documents) are stored on Cloudflare R2 with encryption at rest, served via media.wisrs.com
  • The corporate website is hosted on GitHub Pages
  • Form submissions are processed via Formspree
  • Email is hosted on Zoho Mail (EU/UK data centres)

6.2 Security Measures

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption on all connections (enforced via Cloudflare and Certbot)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt -- we never store plaintext passwords
  • Direct messages are end-to-end encrypted using X25519/XSalsa20-Poly1305 (see Section 12)
  • Database backups every 6 hours with encrypted offsite storage
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints to prevent brute force attacks
  • Automated security monitoring and error tracking via Sentry
  • SSL certificate monitoring with daily checks
  • EXIF metadata (including GPS location) is automatically stripped from uploaded images
  • Security headers including Content-Security-Policy, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy
  • Regular security testing including SQL injection, XSS, and IDOR checks

10. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access -- Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification -- Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure -- Ask us to delete your personal data (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restrict processing -- Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability -- Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Right to object -- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent -- Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Right not to be subject to automated decision-making -- Request human review of automated decisions that significantly affect you

How to Exercise Your Rights

Wisers users: You can download your data, delete your account, and manage privacy settings directly from Settings within the Wisers app at wisrs.com/settings.

All users: Email gdpr@wisrs.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint -- Telephone: 0303 123 1113

11. Children and Young People

Wisers is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a user is under 13, we will delete their account and associated data immediately.

Users aged 13 to 17 may use Wisers, but we recommend parental awareness. We collect date of birth at registration to verify age. Under the UK Online Safety Act, we are working to implement additional age assurance measures to protect young people from harmful content.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data without your consent, please contact us at gdpr@wisrs.com.

12. End-to-End Encryption

Direct messages on Wisers are end-to-end encrypted using the X25519 key exchange and XSalsa20-Poly1305 authenticated encryption (NaCl/libsodium).

This means:

  • Your messages are encrypted on your device before being sent to our servers
  • Only the intended recipient can decrypt and read your messages
  • Balancewise Ltd cannot read the content of your encrypted messages -- we only store ciphertext
  • File attachments in messages are encrypted using the same mechanism

You can set a recovery passphrase to back up your encryption keys. If you lose your keys and have not set a recovery passphrase, your encrypted message history cannot be recovered.

13. Artificial Intelligence

We use artificial intelligence in the following ways:

  • BSCAN AI Fix Suggestions: When you scan a website, you can request AI-generated code fixes for issues found. The AI analyses the scan results and generates suggestions. Your website content is processed by a third-party AI provider (Anthropic) to generate these fixes.
  • Wisers AI Coach: An AI-powered assistant that provides wealth-building guidance. Conversations are processed by a third-party AI provider.
  • Content recommendations: We use algorithmic ranking to show relevant posts in your feed. This is not AI in the regulatory sense -- it is rule-based sorting by engagement, recency and community membership.

Where AI is used, we clearly label it as AI-generated content. Under the EU AI Act, our AI features are classified as limited-risk, requiring transparency but not conformity assessments.

We do not use your personal data to train AI models. We do not use AI for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.
