Meet Chisom Kalu

The person behind Balancewise Technologies — building trusted, modern IT services for businesses across the United Kingdom.

Founder & Lead Engineer
Chisom Kalu

Computer Science student at Northumbria University and founder of Balancewise Ltd. Chisom builds and ships real software — from full CRUD REST APIs in PHP and MySQL to Python automation scripts, cloud infrastructure, and the tools that power Balancewise today, including BSCAN. He holds a Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals certification and runs three business divisions alongside his degree, managing everything from cloud systems and professional email infrastructure to e-commerce operations. Comfortable across the full stack, quick to pick up new technologies, and always building.

BSCAN — Free Website
Audit Tool

BSCAN is a website audit tool built in-house by Balancewise Technologies. Paste any URL and receive an instant report covering SEO, performance, accessibility, and security — with actionable fixes you can apply straight away. It's free to use (3 scans included), with Pro and Agency plans for teams that need more power. Every report is designed to give you honest, practical insights — no fluff, no upselling nonsense.
